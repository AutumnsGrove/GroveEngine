Fundamentally: a short, spontaneous expression. You open Meadow, type a thought,
and it appears in the feed. No blog post. No title required. No featured image.
Just words in the clearing.

It's NOT:

- A blog post (that's a Bloom)

It IS:

- Personal expression without the weight of "publishing"

### The Key Distinction
